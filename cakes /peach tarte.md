# PEACH TARTE

## Ingredients
- Puff Pastry Dough  (or 1 sheet of Pepperidge Farm brand), ½ pound
- 1 Egg  (beaten well with a fork), 
- Fresh Peaches  (about 2 large peaches, or use canned/drained peaches), ½ pound
- Granulated Sugar, 2 tablespoons
- Purpose Flour  (all), 1 tablespoon
- Cinnamon, ¼ teaspoon
- Vanilla Extract, ¼ teaspoon
- Almonds  (sliced), ¼ cup
- Powdered Sugar, ½ cup
- Milk  (or add to achieve desired consistency), 1 tablespoon
